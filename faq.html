Frequently Asked Questions

Here are some of the most common questions about [Script] and our services, with a focus on questions related to Tretinoin, one of our key treatment offerings.

What is Script? 

A teledermatology platform founded by board-certified dermatologists. We provide easy access to expert dermatological care through an online service, catering to a range of skin conditions and treatments, with a special focus on Tretinoin-based therapies.

How does tele-dermatology work? 

Teledermatology at Script involves consulting with our board-certified dermatologists via our online platform. You can schedule appointments, receive diagnoses, obtain treatment plans, and have follow-up consultations, All remotely from the comfort of your home.

What is tretinoin and how do I use it?

Tretinoin is a derivative of Vitamin A and is widely used in dermatology for its effectiveness in treating acne, reducing fine lines and wrinkles, and improving skin texture and tone. It promotes skin cell turnover and is often a key ingredient in anti-aging and acne treatments.

Is tretinoin suitable for all skin types?

Tretinoin is beneficial for various skin types, but its suitability depends on Individual skin conditions and sensitivities. It's important to have a consultation with our dermatologists who can provide personalized advice based on your skin’s specific needs.

Can I use tretinoin if I have sensitive skin?

For those with sensitive skin, our dermatologists might recommend a lower concentration of Tretinoin or a specialized product like "Sensi-Tret." It’s designed to deliver the benefits of Tretinoin with a gentler formulation to minimize potential irritation.

Are there side effects I should know about?

Some common side effects of using tretinoin include redness, peeling, dryness, and sensitivity to sunlight. These are typically mild and decrease with continued use as the skin adjusts. Our dermatologists provide guidance on how to minimize and manage these side effects.

How long will it take to see results? 

Results can vary, but most users begin to see improvements in their skin within 6 to 12 weeks of regular use. Our dermatologists can help set realistic expectations based on your individual skin condition and treatment plan.

Can I use other skin care products too? 

Yes, but it's important to use compatible products. Our dermatologists can advise you on the best skincare routine to use alongside Tretinoin to enhance its benefits and minimize any potential irritation.

I'm excited! How can I get started? 

Getting started is easy! Simply visit our website, sign up, and start a consultation. Board-certified dermatologists are ready to assist you with personalized care and treatment plans.

If I have other questions, what should I do?

For more inquiries or assistance, please reach out to us at support@scriptderm.com. We’re here to help with any questions or concerns you may have! We hope these FAQs have been helpful. At Script, we are committed to providing you with expert care and guidance for all your dermatological needs.
