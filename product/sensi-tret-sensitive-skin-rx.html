Key Features

Tretinoin: A derivative of Vitamin A, tretinoin is celebrated for its profound effects on skin texture and tone. It works meticulously to reduce the appearance of fine lines, wrinkles, and uneven skin tone, revealing a more youthful, radiant complexion.

‍Niacinamide: This versatile form of Vitamin B3 complements tretinoin by helping to soothe the skin, reduce redness, and enhance its natural barrier.Truly a hero ingredient, niacinamide aids in balancing sebum production and reduces inflammation to help you achieve your best skin.

Benefits

Youthful Radiance: Regular use of "Sensi-Tret" helps to accelerate skin cell turnover and build collagen, diminishing signs of aging and bringing forth a more vibrant, youthful glow.

‍Improved Texture and Clarity: Experience a visible refinement in skin texture and a reduction in the appearance of imperfections, resulting in a clearer, smoother complexion.

‍Balanced Hydration: Niacinamide aids in maintaining optimal hydration levels, ensuring your skin feels comfortably moisturized without any heaviness.

How it Works

Always follow the instructions of your prescriber and the medication label. Generally, for the best results, apply a pea-sized amount of "Sensi-Tret" to clean, dry skin in the evening. Start by using it 2-3 times a week, gradually increasing frequency as your skin adjusts. Always remember to use sunscreen during the day while using this product. Results may vary.
