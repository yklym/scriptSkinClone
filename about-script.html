Our Mission
Our mission is wrapped up in offering you revolutionary skin care by making dermatology more accessible, efficient, and patient-centric. We aim to provide high-quality, expert services, ensuring that everyone, regardless of their location or lifestyle, has access to the best skin care treatments. Our focus is on leveraging technology to bridge the gap in healthcare accessibility, ensuring every patient receives the care they deserve.
Our Vision
Our vision is to become the leading online dermatology platform globally, renowned for our commitment to excellence, innovation, and compassionate care. We envision a world where quality dermatological care is not a privilege, but a right accessible to all. We strive to continually advance our platform and services, making them the gold standard in teledermatology, setting new benchmarks in healthcare delivery.
Our Values
Expertise: At Script, we value and uphold the highest levels of expertise. Our team of board-certified dermatologists is at the core of our services, providing knowledgeable, evidence-based treatments and advice.

Accessibility: We are dedicated to making dermatological care accessible to everyone. We strive to eliminate barriers to healthcare access, ensuring that our services are available to those who need them, whenever and wherever.

Innovation: Innovation is the driving force behind Script. We are constantly exploring new technologies and methodologies to enhance our services, ensuring that we offer the most advanced care possible.

Patient-Centric Care: Our patients are at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to providing personalized, empathetic care that respects the unique needs and preferences of each individual.

Our Founders


Founded by board-certified dermatologists, our platform is dedicated to providing affordable and immediate access to high-quality dermatological care with highly effective treatment options. We strive to eliminate the long wait times often associated with in-person dermatologist appointments, ensuring that patients receive timely and accurate diagnoses and treatments from the comfort of their homes.

Dr. Luke Maxfield

Dr. Maxfield is a board-certified dermatologist and fellowship-trained Mohs Micrographic Surgeon with a passion for empowering people to take care of their skin. Over the years, through extensive medical education and interactions with thousands of patients, as well as observing the widespread demand for skin care information and treatment on social media, he identified a significant barrier: access to effective ingredients, quality products, and expert care. While the highest quality care is undoubtedly provided in the office environment, for those unable to see a dermatologist in person, Dr. Maxfield co-founded Script as an online dermatology platform to alleviate this burden and fulfill his passion for enabling everyone to be intentional about their skin health.

Dr. Jarett Casale

Dr. Casale is a dermatologist based in North Carolina with a profound passion for making dermatological care accessible and affordable. Recognizing the challenges many patients face in accessing timely dermatological services, Dr. Casale co-founded Script as an online dermatology platform to bridge this gap. His goal is to connect patients with board-certified dermatologists, providing highly effective medications for common skin conditions that can be diagnosed through teledermatology. This innovative approach eliminates the often months-long wait times for in-person appointments, ensuring that patients receive the care they need promptly and efficiently.

While our team is dedicated to helping you achieve healthy skin, we understand that true health goes beyond the surface. Dr. Maxfield, an avid surfer and competitive chess player, advocates for the holistic engagement of body and mind. Dr. Casale, an outdoor enthusiast, hiker, glass sculptor, and nutrition expert, shares a passion for promoting well-being and lifelong learning. Together, we are committed to enhancing healthcare access and improving lives through comprehensive care and personal empowerment.
